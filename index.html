FAQs

What is LiquidChain in simple terms?
LiquidChain is a next-generation Layer 3 blockchain network designed to connect major ecosystems like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana. It acts as a unified layer that lets assets and liquidity flow seamlessly across these blockchains.
What problem does LiquidChain solve?
Today, most blockchains operate in isolation — making it difficult and costly to move assets or share liquidity between networks. LiquidChain bridges this gap by creating a single layer where users, developers, and investors can interact across multiple blockchains with faster speeds and lower fees.
Why is LiquidChain important?
By merging the power of leading networks, LiquidChain opens the door to a truly connected crypto economy. It helps users access more liquidity, enables developers to build cross-chain applications, and supports a smoother experience for everyday crypto transactions.
What is the purpose of the $LIQUID token?
The $LIQUID token fuels the entire LiquidChain Layer 3 ecosystem. It’s used for network participation, governance, staking, and accessing exclusive features within the platform — making it the foundation of activity and value creation on the chain.
